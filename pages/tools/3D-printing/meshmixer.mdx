# Meshmixer

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/VOcCHYO-9uo" height="430" width="100%" />

Meshmixer is a tool for easy 3D mashups and remixes with specialised tools for 3D printing and repair.

Meshmixer is a fantastic free software for creating and manipulating 3D files for 3D printing. Whether you need to clean up a 3D scan, do some 3D printing or combine multiple 3D models to create a remixed design, Meshmixer can help.

## Supported filetypes

STL (best for 3D printing), OBJ, PLY, AMF, MIX

## Minimum system requirements

At least 4GB of RAM. At least 700 MB of available hard-disk space. Intel HD or similar graphics card with at least 512 MB video memory
