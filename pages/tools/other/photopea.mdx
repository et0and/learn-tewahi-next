# Photopea Basics

[Photopea](https://www.photopea.com/) is a free online image editor that allows you to create and edit digital images. It has many of the same features as Adobe Photoshop, making it a great alternative for those who do not have access to Photoshop or prefer an online editor.

To get started with Photopea, go to their website and click **Create a new project**.
